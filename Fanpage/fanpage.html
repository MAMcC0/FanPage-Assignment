Harry Styles Fan Page

Biography

Harry Styles began his musical career in 2010 when he auditioned as a solo artist on the British television series The X-Factor. Styles did not make it as a solo artist in the series but was rather grouped up into what would become the boyband group One Direction. In 2016, the group went on a hiatus and Styles had his solo debut in 2017.
